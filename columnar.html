Level 3: Columnar Transposition

This is a columnar transposition cipher. The plaintext was written in rows under the keyword CIPHER, then the columns were read in alphabetical order of the keyword letters (C, E, H, I, P, R).

The cipher text is shown below. Your job is to figure out what the original sentence was.

TONRR ELSSI HAEGE XNTPE GATXR SCSAS EXAII RNSTX POERM ERX
